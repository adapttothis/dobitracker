    About This Project

    As a student at UTeM, I noticed it was hard to track exactly how much balance is left on our laundry cards after a few washes. I built Satria Dobi Tracker to solve that. It's a privacy-first, offline tool to help us manage our laundry budget better.

    How to Use

      Note: All data is stored locally on your phone. Clearing your browser cache will reset your balance. Data does not carry over between devices.
